Tidurdikit – Sleep Less, Build More

Welcome to Tidurdikit! We're passionate about making AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're just curious about IoT or eager to see how AI can change the way we interact with technology, you're in the right place. We simplify complex topics and provide free, easy-to-follow tutorials so you can start building and learning without needing any fancy gear or experience. Our motto, “Sleep Less, Build More,” is a reminder to keep pushing ourselves to learn, create, and grow every day. Whether you're new to tech or ready to dive deeper, Tidurdikit is here to show you that tech can be fun, practical, and for everyone. Let’s learn, build, and innovate together!
